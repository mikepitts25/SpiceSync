# App Icon and Launch Screen Design

## Goal

Create a polished SpiceSync app icon and a simple solid-color launch screen.

## Approved Direction

Use two interlocking hearts as the app icon mark. The hearts should look custom and refined, with fuller lobes, a clean taper, pink and purple gloss, a subtle overlap, a restrained sync glow, and one small sparkle. The design should feel romantic and private without becoming explicit or busy.

## Assets

- Replace `apps/mobile/assets/icon.png` with a 1024x1024 full-background icon.
- Replace `apps/mobile/assets/adaptive-icon.png` with a 1024x1024 transparent Android adaptive foreground.
- Replace `apps/mobile/assets/splash-logo.png` with a transparent placeholder so the launch screen reads as a solid color.
- Keep `apps/mobile/app.json` splash and adaptive icon background color at `#0D0006`.

## Verification

Confirm generated PNG dimensions and inspect the icon visually before completion.
