## Core Value Proposition

SpiceSync helps couples discover shared desires, communicate boundaries safely, and explore new experiences together through a private, judgment-free platform designed to reduce friction in intimate conversations.

---

## Key Features (Current/Planned)

### 1. **Desire Discovery Engine**
- Individual preference quiz for each partner
- Covers interests, fantasies, boundaries, and "maybe" zones
- Categories: Activities, settings, roleplay scenarios, toys/props, power dynamics, etc.
- Privacy-first: answers are private until matched

### 2. **The Match Reveal**
- Only shows desires where BOTH partners expressed interest
- Hides mismatches and "no" responses completely
- Creates safe space for vulnerability without fear of judgment
- Generates shared "yes" list as starting point for exploration

### 3. **Spice Level Calibration**
- Users set their comfort/experience level (Mild → Medium → Hot)
- Questions and suggestions adapt to selected level
- Partners can have different levels; app finds overlapping safe zones

### 4. **Conversation Starters**
- Guided prompts based on matched interests
- Icebreakers for discussing new experiences
- Tips for communication and consent

### 5. **Idea Bank / Bucket List**
- Save matched interests for later
- Track experiences you've tried
- Rate and journal about experiences (private or shared)

### 6. **Fantasy Generator / Randomizer**
- "Surprise me" feature based on shared matches
- Date night idea generator
- Seasonal/themed suggestion packs

### 7. **Privacy & Security**
- End-to-end encryption for all data
- No social features, no public profiles
- Optional: Biometric lock, decoy mode
- Data never sold or shared

### 8. **Relationship Check-ins**
- Periodic prompts to revisit preferences (people change!)
- Satisfaction tracking
- Open communication reminders
